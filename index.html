FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance for regular dining, and up to 8 weeks for the Chef's Table experience or private events.
What is the dress code?
We require smart elegant attire for all guests. Formal business wear or elegant evening dress is recommended. We kindly ask guests to avoid casual sportswear.
Do you accommodate dietary requirements?
Absolutely. Our culinary team can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy requirements. Please inform us at the time of booking.
Is there a cancellation policy?
Reservations can be cancelled up to 48 hours before without charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee of $75 per guest.
Can I host a private event at Maison Noir?
Yes — we offer exclusive hire of our private dining rooms and the full restaurant for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations. Contact our events team for a bespoke proposal.
What is the Chef's Table experience?
The Chef's Table is an intimate 12-course tasting menu experience prepared personally by Chef Alexandre Dumont, with front-row views of our kitchen. Limited to 6 guests per evening.
